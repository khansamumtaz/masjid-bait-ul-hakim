About the Masjid

A Place of Peace
& Worship

مسجد بیت الحاکم

مسجد بیت الحاکم is being constructed to serve the Muslim community as a center for Salah, Islamic education, and spiritual growth. Every brick laid is Sadaqah Jariyah — a continuing charity that will benefit the community for generations.

2,000 Worshippers

Spacious prayer hall with separate areas for brothers and sisters.

Quran Education

Dedicated madrasa rooms to study Quran and Islamic sciences.

Community Welfare

Space for events, nikah ceremonies, and social programs.

Sadaqah Jariyah

A continuing charity whose reward lasts long after we are gone.

What We Stand For
Three Pillars of Masjid Bait ul Hakim
01
الإيمان

Faith (Iman)

Rooted in the pure worship of Allah, مسجد بیت الحاکم is built as a house of sincere devotion — where every prayer offered strengthens the bond between the believer and their Creator.

02
العلم

Knowledge (Ilm)

Dedicated madrasa rooms and learning spaces ensure the Quran and Sunnah are passed from generation to generation — nurturing scholars, students, and families of the Ummah.

03
الأمة

Community (Ummah)

A sanctuary open to all — for nikah ceremonies, community programs, and shared celebration. Here, every neighbour is treated as family and no soul faces hardship alone.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To establish مسجد بیت الحاکم as a beacon of faith, knowledge, and unity in Lahore — a sanctuary where every soul finds peace, every child learns the Quran, and every neighbour is treated as family.

Our Mission

To build and sustain a fully-equipped house of worship offering daily prayers, Islamic education, nikah services, and community welfare — funded entirely through the generosity and Sadaqah Jariyah of donors like you.
