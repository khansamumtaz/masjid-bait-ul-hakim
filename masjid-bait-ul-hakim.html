مسجد بیت الحاکم

مسجد بیت الحاکم is being constructed to serve the Muslim community as a center for Salah, Islamic education, and spiritual growth. Every brick laid is Sadaqah Jariyah — a continuing charity that will benefit the community for generations.

🕌

Capacity for 2000 Worshippers

Spacious prayer hall designed for the entire community, with separate areas for brothers and sisters.

📚

Quran & Islamic Education

Dedicated madrasa and learning rooms for children and adults to study Quran and Islamic sciences.

🤝

Community Welfare

Space for community events, nikah ceremonies, and social programs for the ummah.

What We Offer

Masjid Services

🕌

Five Daily Prayers

Jamaat prayers held at the temporary prayer site daily. Full schedule maintained with Adhan and Iqama.

📖

Quran Education

Quran classes for children (ages 5-15) every evening after Maghrib. Tajweed, Hifz, and Nazira programs.

💍

Nikah Services

Nikah ceremonies conducted by certified Islamic scholars. Prior booking required.

🎓

Islamic Lectures

Weekly dars after Fajr and Isha. Monthly scholars' programs and special events during Ramadan.

🤝

Community Support

Zakat distribution, food assistance for needy families, and welfare programs run by the masjid committee.

🌙

Ramadan Programs

Iftaar arrangements, Taraweeh prayers, I'tikaf, and Laylat-ul-Qadr special programs every Ramadan.
