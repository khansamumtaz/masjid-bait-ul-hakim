Our Services

Masjid Bait ul Hakim serves the community with spiritual, educational, and welfare programs — all in the way of Allah.

What We Offer

Masjid Services

Every service we provide is rooted in the Sunnah and aimed at strengthening our community's connection with Allah and with each other.

🕌
پنج وقتہ نماز

Five Daily Prayers (Salah)

All five Fard prayers — Fajr, Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha — are offered in congregation every day. Jumu'ah is held with a weekly Khutbah by our resident Imam. Sisters are welcome in the designated prayer area.

📖
تعلیمِ قرآن

Quran Education

Classes run every evening after Maghrib for children aged 5–15, taught by qualified Hafiz-e-Quran teachers. We offer three tracks — Nazira, Tajweed, and Hifz — in a warm, encouraging environment.

💍
نکاح خدمات

Nikah Services

Nikah ceremonies are conducted by our certified scholars in full accordance with Shariah — simple, dignified, and Sunnah-based. The service includes Khutbat-un-Nikah, Ijab & Qabool, Mahr agreement, and Du'a for the couple.

What's included
  • ✦ Khutbat-un-Nikah
  • ✦ Formal Ijab & Qabool
  • ✦ Mahr agreement
  • ✦ Du'a for the couple
Requirements
  • ✦ Book 7 days in advance
  • ✦ CNIC copies of both parties
  • ✦ Two male witnesses
  • ✦ Contact via WhatsApp

🎓
دینی بیانات و دروس

Islamic Lectures & Dars

Structured Dars after Fajr and Isha every week, covering Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, and the Seerah. Monthly special programs feature renowned scholars — open to men, women, and youth. Past lectures available in our Bayanaat section.

🤝
فلاحِ عامہ

Community Support & Welfare

Our welfare committee distributes Zakat and Sadaqah to deserving families and runs a food assistance program providing ration packages to those in need. Scholarship support for underprivileged students is also available through donor contributions.

🌙
رمضان المبارک پروگرام

Ramadan Programs

Masjid Bait ul Hakim runs a full Ramadan programme including daily Iftaar for all, nightly Taraweeh with full Quran khatam, I'tikaf in the last ten nights, and a special Laylat-ul-Qadr program on the 27th night.

🕊️
نمازِ جنازہ

Janazah (Funeral) Services

Salat-ul-Janazah is offered at the masjid for community members, led by our Imam. We also guide families on Islamic burial rites — ghusl, kafan, and the procession — in accordance with the Sunnah. Participation in Janazah is a Fard Kifayah.
